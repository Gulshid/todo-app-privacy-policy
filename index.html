Todo App — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 4, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Todo App ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal information from users of this application. The App is designed with a privacy-first approach — all data you create remains exclusively on your device.

No personal data collected (name, email, phone, or identifiers)
No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
No location data accessed or stored
No advertising identifiers used

Local data storage

All tasks, notes, and content you create within the App are stored locally on your device using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

Third-party services

The Todo App does not integrate with any third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers. We do not share any information with external parties because no information is collected in the first place.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your region). Since we collect no data from any users, no special risk exists for younger users.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any modifications constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
